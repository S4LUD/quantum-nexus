   - Max 10 energy total

2. **Build a Node**
   - Pay energy cost
   - Add to network
   - Gain efficiency points
   - Generate output

   - Set aside for later (max 3)
   - Gain 1 Flux energy

   - Meet output requirements
   - Actively claim
   - Gain efficiency + ongoing effect
